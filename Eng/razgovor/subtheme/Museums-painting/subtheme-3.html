What kind of painting do you prefer? Why?

I am truly, completely attracted towards abstract art. I would anytime prefer abstract art over realism. Prefer as in, I would first learn, study and try to gain mastery over abstract art before going for realism. Not that I dislike realism, but it's the abstract paintings which are more captivating for me.

Why do I prefer abstract art?
Abstract paintings are all about the game of colours. I get an adrenaline rush whenever I look at colours. Abstracts are about creating something which is aesthetically good, visually pleasing just out colours, strokes, shapes may be and textures. There is no definite subject, no definite/fixed message. Although there has to be a firm foundation of shapes/colours to create a good piece of abstract art. According to me, to create something visually pleasing just out of colours and textures in a limited time is a lot more difficult than spending ample amount of time on detailing as in realism. I don't intend to say that realism is easy. It's just that more emphasis is on technique and minute detailing. Realism is good in it's own way.
