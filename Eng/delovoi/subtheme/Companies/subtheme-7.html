Requirements for the potential employee

A potential employee must own certain knowledge and skills. For example, this list may include:
· ability to think innovatively and practically
· high degree of business awareness
· good communication skills
· ambitions
· wide range of interests and experience
· work under pressure

In addition, there may be other requirements. The list depends on the desired position. The employee should write information about his knowledge, skills and abilities in his CV. Based on the data received, the employer makes a decision on employ an employee.
