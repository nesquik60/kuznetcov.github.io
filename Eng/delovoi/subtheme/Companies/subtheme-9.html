Business sector

A business sector is a multitude of activities for a specific business. The list of sectors is determined by the field in which the company operates which products it produces. The list of organization sectors includes: Manufacturing, food retailing, real estate and agriculture, other sectors. This is the sector of the company Edizone holding. Also, I can use Sony as an example. The business sectors of this company include: electronics, games, music, pictures, insurance, and others.
