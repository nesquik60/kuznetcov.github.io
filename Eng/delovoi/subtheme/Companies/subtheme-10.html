What is brand name? Popular brand names.

The brand is a name a company gives to its products so they can easily recognized. The brand name can be not only the name of the product or service, but also the name of the store, and the name of the manufacturer. This is what the consumer notices in the first place, and what influences the formation of the relationship to the brand. There are well-known brand names in every area of business. For example, in the field of cosmetics and perfumes famous brand names are Kenzo, Givenchy, Christian Dior and others. In the field of electronics such as Apple, LG, Philips. In the field of sportswear brands include Nike, Adidas, Puma.
