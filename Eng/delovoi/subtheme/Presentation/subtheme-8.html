Your tips of advice for making a perfect presentation

I believe that the whole presentation should be of high quality. For this, all possible methods must be applied. I think that it is necessary to pay attention to every point of the presentation.

I can say several main things in my opinion:
1) good preparation on the subject
2) repetition of material before presentation
3) equipment check
4) talk more than read
5) use visual support
6) and of course a clear structure

But we must not forget about all the other rules of a good presentation.
