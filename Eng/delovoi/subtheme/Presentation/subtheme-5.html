Presentatio structure

Any presentation should have a clear structure. It concludes introduction, middle and end of presentation.
-introduction: 1)greeting
2)Subject
3) Structure
4) Timing
5)Policy on questions/ discussion

-middle (the main body)
The main body of the presentation contains the details of the subject or these described in the introduction. In main presentation the main body can be usefull divided into different parts.
Signalling different parts in the presentation:
Ending the introduction
Beginning the main body
Listing
Linking : ending parts within main body
Linking: beginning a new part
Sequencing
-The end of the presentation:

Ending the main body of the presentation
Beginning the summary and/ or conclusion
Concluding
Inviting questions and/ or introducing discussion
