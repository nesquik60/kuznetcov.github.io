Tips for applicants:

Qualities:
· Intelligence
· Character
· Ability to react
· Suitability to the position

Appearance:
· Tidy
· Good manners
· Well-presented
· Pleasant

Things candidate is expected to know:
· Good knowledge of what the company does
· What he is expected to do
· Who is he going to report to

Mistakes:
· Agree with everything potential employer says
· Lack of chemistry between candidate and employer

Advice:
· To listen to potential employer attentively
· To ask appropriate questions
· To create good relationships with the interviewer
