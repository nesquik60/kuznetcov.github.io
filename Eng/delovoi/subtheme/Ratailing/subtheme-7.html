Advantages and disadvantages of direct and indirect shopping

Direct shopping:
o Advantages:
· You can see and buy any thing you want in real time
· If you buy clothes, you can try it on
· You can check the product on production rejects

o Disadvantages:
· There are checkout lines to worry about
· There are no promo codes for the discount
· You had to loose time for searching goods
· Limited working hours of shops
· You can run into fake (нарваться на подделку)

Indirect shopping:
o Advantages:
· It saves your time, you can buy goods don’t get off the couch
· There are sales and different promo codes on discounts in Internet often
· You can order goods and services which you can’t find in real time
· It is easy to compare prices
· You can read reviews of real people on goods before purchase

o Disadvantages:
· Commodity is out of stoke often
· You had to wait goods
· Paid delivery
· You can’t check goods
· You can face frauds
