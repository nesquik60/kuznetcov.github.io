Are exercises enough to keep fit and healthy?

We all know that exercise is important in our daily lives. Our bodies are designed and have evolved to be regularly active. There are many benefits of regular exercise and maintaining fitness and these include: increasing energy levels, improving muscle strength, helping you to maintain a healthy weight, improving brain function, enhancing your immune system, improving your mood etc. But you cannot merely exercise and expect your body to stay fit. A balanced diet is also essential for that. Healthy eating protects you against many chronic diseases, such as heart disease, diabetes and cancer. So, in order to be heathy and fit you should do both physical exercises and follow the diet rules.
